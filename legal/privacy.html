Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 31, 2025

DaddyFreud ("App") is operated by Zahui Varga Erik ("Developer/We"), based in Romania. For the purposes of the GDPR, the Developer is the Data Controller.

We practice "Data Minimization" to protect your privacy.

1. Data We Collect (And Don't Collect)

1.1 Anonymous Identifier

We generate a random UUID (UserIdentityService) to identify your device. We do not collect your email address or phone number for standard use.

1.2 Chat Content

Your messages are processed to generate AI responses. Under GDPR (Article 9), mental health discussions are Special Category Data. By using the App, you explicitly consent to this processing for the purpose of providing the AI companion service.

1.3 Emotion Data (Text-Only)

Important: We analyze your messages using Natural Language Processing (NLP) to detect sentiment (e.g., "Sad", "Happy").

WE DO NOT USE BIOMETRIC DATA. We do not record your voice, analyze your facial geometry, or use "emotion recognition" as defined under the EU AI Act's high-risk categories.

Our AI analyzes the words you type, not biological characteristics.

1.4 Addiction Tracking Data

If you enable sync, encrypted logs of your addiction tracking (e.g., "Relapse Events") are stored in our secure database (Supabase) tied to your anonymous ID.

1.5 Technical Data

  • Device model and operating system version
  • Crash logs (via Supabase/Apple/Google)
  • IP address (for security and abuse prevention)

2. How We Process Your Data

2.1 AI Inference (OpenRouter)

Your anonymized message text is sent to third-party AI providers (e.g., Google DeepMind, Google A.I, Alibaba AI group) via OpenRouter solely to generate a response.

These providers are contractually prohibited from training their models on your data. However, they may retain logs for up to 30 days for abuse monitoring and safety purposes.

2.2 Cloud Sync (Supabase)

If you enable sync, encrypted logs of your addiction tracking (e.g., "Relapse Events") are stored in our secure database (Supabase) tied to your anonymous ID.

  • Encryption: All data is encrypted in transit (TLS) and at rest (AES-256)
  • Location: Supabase servers are located in the EU/US

3. Data Deletion ("The Nuke Button")

You have the right to full data erasure.

3.1 How to Delete

Go to Settings → "Delete Account".

3.2 What Happens

This triggers a local DataDeletionService protocol that:

  • Wipes your Keychain IDs
  • Deletes all local chat logs and cache
  • Deletes your remote data on Supabase (if found)

3.3 Result

This action is irreversible. Because we do not have your email, we cannot restore your account after you click this button.

4. Quality Improvement & Human Review (Optional)

While most interactions are fully automated, we employ a human review system to improve clinical safety and service quality.

  • When It Happens: If you flag a response as unhelpful or unsafe, the interaction may be queued for review.
  • Privacy Protection: A verified clinical reviewer may view an anonymized summary of your situation. They do not see your raw chat logs, name, or other personal identifiers.
  • Real-Time Support: The reviewer may provide an improved response.
  • Service Improvement: Anonymized interaction patterns help us train and improve future AI models.

5. Data Storage & Security

  • Database: User data is stored securely using Supabase (servers located in the EU/US).
  • Encryption: All data is encrypted in transit (TLS) and at rest (AES-256).
  • Local Processing: Where possible, logic is handled locally on your device.

6. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal identifiable data. We share data with the following parties:

Infrastructure Providers:

  1. OpenRouter: For AI text generation.
  2. Supabase: For database hosting and authentication.
  3. Stripe/Apple: For payment processing.

Research & Development:

We may aggregate and de-identify data to support research and development efforts in mental health technology. This may include sharing anonymized insights with research partners and academic institutions to advance the field of mental health AI. All such data is stripped of personal identifiers and contains no information that could reasonably be used to identify you.

7. Your Rights (GDPR)

You have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of all data we hold about you.
  • Erasure: Delete your account instantly inside the App settings. This is permanent.
  • Rectification: Correct wrong information.
  • Portability: Export your journal logs.

To exercise these rights, contact: askdaddyfreud@gmail.com

8. International Transfers

Your data may be processed on servers in the United States. By using the App, you consent to this transfer.

We rely on Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs) for data transfers from the EU/UK to ensure your data remains protected under GDPR standards.

9. Data Retention

We retain your data only as long as necessary to provide our services. When you delete your account via the "Nuke Button," all personal data is permanently removed from our active systems within 30 days.

10. Children's Privacy

The App is not intended for children under 13 (or 16 in the EU/UK). We do not knowingly collect data from minors.

If we discover a user is underage, we will delete their data immediately.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. We will notify you of material changes via email or through the App at least 30 days before they take effect. Continued use after changes constitutes acceptance.
