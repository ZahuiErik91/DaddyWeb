Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 30, 2025

1. Who We Are

DaddyFreud ("App") is a personal project operated by Zahui Varga Erik ("Developer/We"), based in Romania. For the purposes of the GDPR, the Developer is the Data Controller.

2. Special Category Data (Mental Health)

The App is designed to help you track sensitive information regarding your mood, addiction recovery, and mental state. Under GDPR (Article 9), this is Special Category Data.

By using the App, you explicitly consent to the processing of this sensitive data for the sole purpose of providing the tracking and AI coaching services. You may withdraw this consent at any time by deleting your account.

3. Data We Collect

3.1 Sensitive Health Data

Special Category Data: The contents of your conversations with DaddyFreud, including discussions about mental health, addiction recovery, and emotional wellbeing, are classified as Sensitive Personal Data under GDPR Article 9 and similar regulations. We collect this data only with your explicit, opt-in consent for the purpose of providing the therapy support service.

3.2 Other Data Types

  • Account Information: Email address, username, password (encrypted).
  • Usage Data: Chat logs, journal entries, sobriety clocks, mood ratings.
  • Identity Verification Data: If you apply for "Therapist Mode," we may collect proof of ID or professional licensure via secure third-party processors (e.g., Stripe Identity). We do not store raw ID images after verification.
  • Technical Data: IP address, device model, browser type, crash logs (via Supabase/Apple).

3.3 Biometric Data Exclusion (EU AI Act Compliance)

Important Clarification: DaddyFreud utilizes text-based Natural Language Processing (NLP) to understand sentiment and emotional context from the semantic content of your messages.

We DO NOT collect, store, or process:

  • Voiceprints or voice biometric templates
  • Facial geometry or facial recognition data
  • Keystroke dynamics or typing rhythm patterns
  • Any other biometric identifiers for emotion recognition

Our AI analyzes the words you type, not biological or behavioral characteristics. This approach ensures we are not classified as a "biometric emotion recognition system" under the EU AI Act.

4. How We Use Your Data

4.1 Third-Party AI Processing

To function, the AI sends your chat text to OpenRouter (which routes to models like Anthropic Claude, OpenAI GPT, or Google Gemini).

  • No Training by Third Parties: We have configured our API settings to request that these providers DO NOT use your data to train their models. However, they may retain logs for up to 30 days for abuse monitoring.
  • Data Minimization: We send only the conversation history necessary for the immediate context.

4.2 AI Model Training & Improvement (Opt-In Only)

Default State: Your chat logs are NOT used to train our AI models by default. They are used solely to generate responses in your active session.

Opt-In for Service Improvement: You may choose to opt-in to help improve DaddyFreud. If you do:

  • Your conversation logs will be de-identified (all personal identifiers stripped)
  • Anonymized data may be used to fine-tune our models for better accuracy and empathy
  • You can revoke this consent at any time in Settings
  • Upon revocation, your data will be removed from future training sets (though it cannot be removed from models already trained)

4-B. Quality Improvement & Human Review

While most interactions are fully automated, we employ a human review system to improve clinical safety and service quality.

  • When It Happens: If you flag a response as unhelpful or unsafe, or if our automated systems detect a complex scenario, the interaction may be queued for review.
  • Privacy Protection: A verified clinical reviewer may view an anonymized summary of your situation. They do not see your raw chat logs, name, or other personal identifiers.
  • Real-Time Support: The reviewer may provide an improved response to better support you.
  • Service Improvement: Anonymized interaction patterns and corrected responses help us train and improve future versions of our AI models. This process is similar to how other AI services improve over time.

5. Data Storage & Security

  • Database: User data is stored securely using Supabase (servers located in the EU/US).
  • Encryption: All data is encrypted in transit (TLS) and at rest (AES-256).
  • Local Processing: Where possible, logic is handled locally on your device.

6. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal identifiable data. We share data with the following parties:

Infrastructure Providers:

  1. OpenRouter: For AI text generation.
  2. Supabase: For database hosting and authentication.
  3. Stripe/Apple: For payment processing.

Research & Development:

We may aggregate and de-identify data to support research and development efforts in mental health technology. This may include sharing anonymized insights with research partners and academic institutions to advance the field of mental health AI. All such data is stripped of personal identifiers and contains no information that could reasonably be used to identify you.

7. Your Rights (GDPR)

You have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of all data we hold about you.
  • Erasure: Delete your account instantly inside the App settings. This is permanent.
  • Rectification: Correct wrong information.
  • Portability: Export your journal logs.

To exercise these rights, contact: askdaddyfreud@gmail.com

8. International Transfers

If you are located in the EEA, note that some of our processors (e.g., OpenRouter, Stripe) are based in the USA. We rely on the Data Privacy Framework (DPF) or Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs) to ensure your data remains protected.

9. Data Retention

We retain your data only as long as necessary to provide our services or as required by law. When you delete your account, all personal data is permanently removed from our active systems within 30 days.

10. Children's Privacy

The App is not intended for use by individuals under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal data from minors. If you believe we have inadvertently collected such information, please contact us immediately.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. We will notify you of material changes via email or through the App at least 30 days before they take effect. Continued use after changes constitutes acceptance.
